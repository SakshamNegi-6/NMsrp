Official Regulations

Core Rules

  • FailRP: Unrealistic actions (e.g., flipping car mid-air).
  • FearRP: Value your life when threatened (gun to head).
  • NLR: Forget last 15 mins after death. Do not return to scene.
  • Metagaming: Using OOC info (Discord/Stream) in-game.
  • Powergaming: Forcing RP on others without giving them a chance to react.

Criminal Limits

  • Mafia: Max 4 members.
  • Bank Heist: Min 2 Cops online.
  • Priority Cooldown: 15 mins between major crimes.
  • Kidnapping: Requires staff ticket approval.

Community Guidelines

  • No Mini-Modding: Let staff handle issues.
  • Advertising: DM ads = Permanent Ban.
  • Respect: Zero tolerance for toxicity/slurs.

LSPD & BCSO

  • LSPD: City limits only.
  • BCSO: County & Sandy Shores.
  • Pursuits: No ramming unless speeds < 50 MPH.
  • Uniforms: Official EUP mandatory.

SAFD & DOT

  • SAFD: Ladder trucks for high-rise calls only.
  • DOT: Cannot tow active scene cars without PD request.
